About

Olospo (ol-os-po) was established in 2011 and is home to Oxford based Designer Tom Brooks.

 

Although Oxford is where I call home, I spent 4 years (2007-2011) in the lovely city of Bristol learning the tools of my trade. During that time I graduated with a BSc (Hons) degree in Web Design at the University of the West of England. Straight from graduating I spent a year working for a Print Marketing and Communications company as their in-house designer, working with clients such as Diageo, Serco, Comet and Bristol City Council. Since setting up Olospo I have had the pleasure of working with numerous small businesses and individuals as well as various agency projects for companies such as Shire, POhWER and Yahoo!
